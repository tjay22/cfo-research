The 2018 Global Business & Spending Outlook


The cross-industry findings in the joint American Express and Institutional Investor study are based on a survey of 870 CFOs and other senior finance executives at companies with annual revenue of $500 million or more from more than 21 countries worldwide. And for the first time in its 11-year history, the study includes respondents from the Middle East region – particularly the UAE, Egypt, Saudi Arabia, and Qatar – adding to its holistic view of a dynamic global services economy.

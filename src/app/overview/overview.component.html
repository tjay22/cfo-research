The 2018 Global Business & Spending Outlook

The 2018 Global Business & Spending Outlook details Middle East’s executives’ views on the economic outlook, spending plans, and the future of technology.

The cross-industry findings in the joint American Express and Institutional Investor¹ study are based on a survey of 870 CFOs and other senior finance executives at companies with annual revenue of $500 million or more from more than 21 countries worldwide. And for the first time in its 11-year history, the study includes respondents from the Middle East region –– adding to its holistic view of a dynamic global services economy.

  • Senior finance executives report great confidence in the economic growth prospects in 2018
  • Region's optimistic climate is expected to boost business spending and investment levels thus shifting businesses in to growth mode
  • In 2018, the top priority for Middle East finance executives is better meeting customers’ evolving needs
  • This year, the focus is on new product development and mobile technology
  • Employments levels are anticipated to rise. More freelancers, part-timers and temps are expected to join Middle East’s growing work force
  • Visionary finance executives are taking great interest in next-generation technologies with investments in Robotics & Automation and AI
